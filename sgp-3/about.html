Welcome to Gotilo!

A project dedicated to revolutionizing hostel management and reviews.

Our Story

This project was born out of a desire to enhance the hostel experience for students.

What We Do

In this website, we provide a comprehensive management and review system specifically for local hostels.

Features

  • Student Management Tools
  • Integrated Review System
  • Community Hub

Our Objective

The primary objectives of this project are: To automate and digitize the hostel management process. To provide an easy and efficient way for students and administrators to manage hostel-related tasks. To enhance transparency in hostel allocation and billing processes. To reduce manual workloads and minimize errors in managing hostel facilities.
